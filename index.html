Suicide Prevention

services that educate individuals and communities about suicide and offer appropriate support for those people who are at risk of Suicide or Bereaved by Suicide.

BACKGROUND

After year 2000 ever rising number of suicides in the State of Jammu & Kashmir became a cause of serious concern not only for the Civil Society but also for the private, public and corporate sector. Nothing concrete was being done to address the problem. There was urgent need to provide a set up a system with trained human resource to work towards creating a suicide free society. In 2012 a dedicated team of people after pondering over the issue, an idea was mooted to set up a Registered NGO namely ‘THE – SARA’.

Today ‘THE – SARA’ is among few trusted grass root NGO’s of India, working on varied issues and trying to improve lives of thousands of people.
